Welcome to San Marcos Pickleball, the premier destination for pickleball enthusiasts in North County San Diego. Our facility was designed from the ground up with one goal in mind: to create the ultimate pickleball experience for players of all ages and skill levels.

Our facility features multiple championship-quality courts, professional lighting, and climate-controlled indoor playing areas. Whether you're looking for competitive league play, casual open court time, or professional instruction, San Marcos Pickleball has everything you need to elevate your game.

Join our growing community of pickleball players and discover why San Marcos Pickleball is quickly becoming the go-to destination for the fastest-growing sport in America. We can't wait to see you on the courts!
